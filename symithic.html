FACTS ABOUT DOGS

Dogs are not only our best friends but also fascinating creatures with unique traits and histories. Here are some interesting facts about dogs that you might not know! General Facts Descendants of Wolves: All dogs are direct descendants of wolves, with domestication dating back around 20,000 to 26,000 years ago. 2 Unique Nose Prints: Just like human fingerprints, each dog's nose has a unique pattern, making it a reliable identifier. 2 Diverse Breeds: Dogs are the most diverse mammals on Earth, ranging from tiny Chihuahuas to massive Great Danes, with a variety of coat types and behaviors. 2 Incredible Sense of Smell: A dog's sense of smell is at least 40 times more sensitive than that of humans, allowing them to detect scents from great distances. 1 Dogs Dream: Just like humans, dogs experience dreams, and puppies and senior dogs tend to dream more frequently than adult dogs. 1
